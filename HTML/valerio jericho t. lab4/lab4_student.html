IVE Foundation Diploma (Information Technology)

This Programme aims to deliver a quality education, with generic and IT knowledge and skills, that will enable students to achieve the skills required for developing a career in the IT industry, and a solid foundation for further studies.


Major IT modules:

  • Programming Concepts and Applications
  • Database Applications Development
  • Computer Systems and Communication Fundamentals
  • Multimedia Website Design Practices

Articulation Path:

  1. Higher Diploma
  2. Undergraduate Programme
  3. Postgraduate Programme
