Jericho Valson    
                           Jericho Valson was born on July in 2002 and grew up in the northwestern coast of Lantau Island, Hong Kong.
  Writing his own songs and a self taught producer, he has been influenced from artists like Frank Sinatra, Logic, The Weeknd, Mac Miller and Adam Lambert.
                             His first release "COGNITION" speaks to these diverse influences.
           The song will be released in April. He has collaborated with MAIAH, MLE, Vxlious and MildSauce.
